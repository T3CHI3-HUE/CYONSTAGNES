About Us

The Catholic Youth Organisation of Nigeria (CYON), St. Agnes Parish, Maryland, Ikeja, Lagos, is a dynamic community of young Catholics dedicated to faith, service, and personal growth. Guided by our motto "Let your light shine" and our slogan "For God and for youths through Christ," we aim to inspire and empower young people to live out their faith boldly and make a positive impact in the Church and society. Under the patronage of Saint Charles Lwanga, our members draw strength from his example of courage and devotion. Our Mission

To build a generation of God-fearing, self-reliant, and purpose-driven youths committed to spreading the light of Christ.
